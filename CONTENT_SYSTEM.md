# GOLDEN RULE

You are not designing a website.

You are directing a cinematic digital experience.

The visitor should feel like they watched a premium environmental documentary.

Every section must naturally lead into the next.

There should never be a feeling of:

"I reached another section."

Instead:

"I am continuing the story."

If something is missing...

DO NOT GUESS.

DO NOT INVENT.

"I used stock imagery."

Correct

----------------------

❌ Wrong

"I wrote marketing copy."

"I need final copy.
